Save The Weather

As the world speed up the pace of industrialization, the unrestrained growth of carbon dioxide emissions on the damage is more and more big, the earth's ozone layer and lead to world climate face more and more problems, climatic disasters frequently occur. In such a situation, the world is paying more and more attention to the issue of climate change, and generally calls for "green GDP" growth mode and "low-carbon life" mode of life. Some people think that carbon emissions is quite a long way from our daily life, there are carbon emissions as long as it is refers to the industrial emissions, actually otherwise, the human body through breathing every day about the release of 1 kg of carbon dioxide, computer use 1 year on average 10.5 kg of carbon dioxide emissions indirectly, we daily use all kinds of electrical appliances, car, or even the use of disposable wooden chopsticks, in emissions of carbon dioxide, thereby reducing carbon emissions in our midst
